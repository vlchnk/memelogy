Meme

Not to be confused with Mime. This article is about the term "meme" in general. For the usage of the term on the internet (or a trend that spreads quickly), see Internet meme. For other uses, see Meme (disambiguation).

A meme (/miÀêm/ MEEM) is an idea, behavior, or style that spreads by means of imitation from person to person within a culture‚Äîoften with the aim of conveying a particular phenomenon, theme, or meaning represented by the meme. A meme acts as a unit for carrying cultural ideas, symbols, or practices, that can be transmitted from one mind to another through writing, speech, gestures, rituals, or other imitable phenomena with a mimicked theme. Supporters of the concept regard memes as cultural analogues to genes in that they self-replicate, mutate, and respond to selective pressures. Wikipedia
